PROPOSAL

This website provides information on plans to build a new film studio and creative arts hub at the famed Harris Ranch in California's Central Valley.

We will champion the creativity of Latinos, women, and other underrepresented groups in the film arts on a national stage.

The studio will grow the local economy, supporting around 350 direct jobs.
